ELEPHANT SHREW

The Elephant Shrew is not actually a shrew at all, It shares closer DNA matches with elephants, aardvarks, and hyraxes.

  • Scientific Name: Macroscelidea
  • Average Length: 20 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 250 g
  • Habitat: Africa

All are four-legged with mouse-like tails, and rather long legs for their size which are used to hop and jump about like rabbits.
